ITBSS History

Driven by a strong desire and desire to participate in the process of educating the nation and is a continuation of the secondary education level that has been organized by Yayasan Gereja Protestan Kampung Bali (YGPKB), which is an extension of the services mandated by the West Kalimantan Christian Church (GKKB) Jemaat Pontianak in the field of education, and with the spirit of Indonesian nationality and unity on October 13, 2019 a Higher Education Compartment (KomDikti) was formed by YGPKB Pontianak with the aim of being an organizer to establish higher education named the Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business (ITBSS).

After going through a long process, thanks to the grace and help of the Most Good God, the Decree of the Minister of Education, Culture, Research and Technology Number 460 / E / O / 2021 concerning Permission to Establish the Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business in Pontianak City, West Kalimantan Province organized by the Yayasan Gereja Protestan Kampung Bali, can be published and handed over directly by the Head of the Higher Education Service Institution XI, Prof. Dr. H. Udiansyah, M.S. on November 11, 2021.
