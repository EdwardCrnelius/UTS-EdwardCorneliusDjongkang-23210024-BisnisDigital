BD Program

What is BD Program?

The Digital Business study program prepares graduates who have superior digipreneur skills and are able to develop and apply qualified digital technology. Students will be equipped with theory-based learning, practice, and modern lecture facilities so that students can get an optimal learning experience. The Digital Business study program has three main concentrations, namely Digital Marketing, Technopreneur, and Digital Business Analyst.

BD Vision

Becoming a Study Program that Produces Competent, Skilled and Superior Digipreneurs Nationally and Globally in accordance with Christian Values.

BD Mission

  1. Organizing Higher Education based on Digital Business Science and Technology to Produce Competent, Skilled and Globally Superior Graduates in accordance with Christian Values.
  2. Organizing Research to contribute to all external and internal stakeholders of the study program.
  3. Digital Business Carry out Community Service that Provides Significant Value and Impact in accordance with Digital Business knowledge.
  4. Establishing Cooperation with Government Institutions and the World of Business / Industry (DU / DI) in the field of Digital Business.

BD Target

  1. Carrying out higher education activities in accordance with the National Higher Education Standards to produce graduates who are skilled and competent in the digital business sector.
  2. Organizing quality research activities with innovative methods and relevant to the latest issues in digital business
  3. Impacting the community through the application of digital business knowledge in solving social and business problems of the community.
  4. Initiate cooperation with potential partners from related agencies and DUDI that can help the development of digital business study programs.

BD Graduated Profile

  1. Excellent work ethics with Christian worldview as the basis for running his life, both personal and professional.
  2. Neat business management concepts. Graduates of this study program will master business management concepts neatly and be able to translate these concepts into appropriate business actions.
  3. The concept of creative thinking and Higher Order Thinking Skills (HOTS) displayed in various business plans and the utilization of digital tools in supporting and succeeding business plans.
  4. Digital entrepreneurial skills, including good and effective communication skills, managing information wisely, innovative digital content creation, data-driven problem solving, and deep knowledge of business security in the digital era.
  5. Global perspective. Graduates of this study program will be quick to respond and carefully analyze global issues related to digital business and how they will utilize global information to create an advantage for their business products.
