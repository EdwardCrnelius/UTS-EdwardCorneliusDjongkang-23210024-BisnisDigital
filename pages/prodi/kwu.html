KWU Program

What is KWU Program?

The Entrepreneurship study program aims to prepare students to become Creativepreneurs who are able to identify and apply new ideas, concepts, and strategies in planning, managing, and developing an enterprise or business.

KWU Graduated Profile

  1. An excellent image of life and ethics with a Christian worldview as the basis for running his life, both personal and professional.
  2. Ability to become a technopreneur. Graduates of the Entrepreneurship Study Program have knowledge and expertise in utilizing the latest technology in supporting the business concepts and strategies initiated.
  3. Higher Order Thinking Skills (HOTS) that are displayed in the presentation of ideas, analysis of concepts and sharp business strategies.
  4. Ability and skills in effective communication in making decisions, selling, negotiating effectively and convincingly.
  5. Global perspective to build local business. Graduates of this study program will be responsive and carefully analyze global issues related to entrepreneurship and develop local potentials to be developed through innovation and creativity so that they have high competitiveness.
